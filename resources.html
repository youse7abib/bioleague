Competition System

Phase 1 is the open-round multiple-choice exam across four biology sections. Then the top 16 teams qualify to Phase 2, a fast-paced match phase with three rounds per team.

Prizes & Medal Status

  • Top 3 teams: “Gold Medal” status.
  • Ranks 4-7: “Silver Medal”.
  • Ranks 8-10: “Bronze Medal”.

Recognition

  • Golden medalists: nomination for the Lumiere research program.
  • Top 10 teams: recommendation letter from the president, validated by academic partners.
  • Phase 2 qualifiers: special certificates with co-organizers’ signature validation.
