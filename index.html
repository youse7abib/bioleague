Competition System

Two phases designed to test breadth, speed, and accuracy across the four biology sections.

Phase 1: Open Round

  • Online open-round (Bio-league) across the four biology sections.
  • The first phase is simply multiple-choice questions on the same four sections.
  • Top teams qualify to Phase 2.

Phase 2: Match Phase

  • Top 16 teams qualify for a fast-paced second phase.
  • Fast-paced questions with three rounds for every team.

Prizes

After Phase 2 ends, medals and recognition are awarded based on team ranking.

Medal Status

  • Top 3 teams earn the “Gold Medal” status.
  • Next 4 teams (ranks 4-7) earn “Silver Medal”.
  • Last 3 teams (ranks 8-10) earn “Bronze Medal”.

Recognition & Certificates

  • Gold medalists receive a nomination for the Lumiere research program.
  • Top 10 teams receive a recommendation letter from the president, validated by academic partners.
  • Special certificates are given to all Phase 2 qualifiers, validated through signatures by co-organizers.
