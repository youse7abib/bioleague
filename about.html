About BioLeague

An international, totally online general biology competition built around four key disciplines.

What is the BioLeague?

BioLeague is a global open-round competition on general biology. It covers: Anatomy & Physiology, Genetics & Evolution, Biochemistry & Cell Biology, and Ecology & Ethology.

  • Designed for both teams and competitive individuals.
  • International participation with online delivery.
  • Two phases: open-round qualification followed by a match phase.

Competition System (Summary)

  • Phase 1 is an open-round multiple-choice exam across the four sections.
  • The top 16 teams qualify to Phase 2.
  • Phase 2 includes fast-paced questions with three rounds per team.

Competition materials are mentioned in the official syllabus files.
